**Question:** What is a CBR test in soil mechanics?
**Answer:** The California Bearing Ratio (CBR) test evaluates the subgrade mechanical strength of soils and base course materials. It measures the pressure required to penetrate a soil sample with a standard 50mm diameter plunger, comparing the resulting force against a standardized reference of high-quality crushed California limestone.
