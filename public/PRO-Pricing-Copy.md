3. **Is it really easy to cancel?**
   Yes. No calling support or jumping through hoops. Go to Settings > Billing and click "Cancel Subscription."
4. **Are the live rates accurate for my specific city?**
   PRO currently pulls daily aggregated wholesale data from major hubs in Pakistan, India, and the UAE. While highly accurate for estimates, always verify final purchase orders with local vendors.

   Absolutely. PRO users can upload their company logo and set custom brand colors via the Settings panel for all exported PDF proposals.
6. **If I buy the Annual plan, do I get updates?**
   Yes! All PRO users automatically receive access to every new calculator, feature, and tool we release entirely for free.
7. **Is my data secure in the cloud?**
   We use enterprise-grade AWS encryption. Your project data, client estimates, and bids are completely private and never shared.
8. **Can I upgrade from PRO to Team later?**
   Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. We will automatically prorate your payment based on what you have already used.
9. **How many computers can I log into with PRO?**
   Your PRO license is tied to your account. You can log in from your office desktop, your laptop, and your mobile phone seamlessly.
